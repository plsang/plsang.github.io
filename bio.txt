Sang Phan is a project researcher at the National Institute of Informatics (NII), Japan. He earned his PhD in Informatics at The Graduate University for Advanced Studies (SOKENDAI) in 2015. Prior to that, he received his BS and MS degrees in 2009 and 2012, respectively, from the University of Science - Vietnam National University Ho Chi Minh City. His research interests include applications of deep learning in computer vision, natural language processing, and multimedia analysis.
